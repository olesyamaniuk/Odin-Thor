  • Is City Jigsaw Puzzles free to play?
    Yes, it's free with optional in-app purchases for bonus content.

    Absolutely! From casual 24-piece to challenging 100+ piece puzzles.

    Yes! Each puzzle is based on real global landmarks and skylines.

    Yes, you can play your downloaded puzzles without an internet connection.

    Yes! Earn passport stamps and unlock new cities with progress.
